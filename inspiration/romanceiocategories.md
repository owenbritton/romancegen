# Romance.io Categories and Subcategories

Comprehensive list of categories and subcategories available on Romance.io for filtering and discovering romance books.

---

## 1. Steam Level (Heat/Spice Level)

The amount of sexual activity and detail in a book.

- **Glimpses and Kisses**
  - Meaningful glances and perhaps a kiss
  - No sex on or off page

- **Behind Closed Doors**
  - At least one intimate scene occurs
  - Fade-to-black (not shown to reader)

- **Open Door**
  - At least one intimate scene with reader present
  - Euphemistic language for acts and body parts
  - Mild to medium spicy content

- **Explicit Open Door**
  - At least two intimate scenes
  - Explicit language with variety of sexual acts
  - Spicy content with explicit details

- **Explicit and Plentiful**
  - Several explicit scenes throughout the book
  - Variety of adventurous acts
  - Very spicy with plenty of sexual activity
